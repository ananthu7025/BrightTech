Our Manufacturing & Automation Services

ISO 9001:2015 certified solutions for design, manufacturing and supply of jigs & fixtures, gauges, welding robot cells, and precision machined components.

Welding Fixtures

Custom designed and manufactured welding fixtures including sub-assemblies, base plates, resting units, clamp arms, and wear plates for various applications.

  • Aluminum welding fixtures
  • SS tube welding setups
  • Rotary & indexing fixtures
Robot Welding Cells

Complete turnkey robot cell solutions including design, manufacturing, installation, and training support for automated welding operations.

  • Robot pedestals & enclosures
  • Rotary holding bases
  • Teaching & installation support
Checking Gauges

Precision checking gauges for quality control with automated features including QR code printing, count tracking, and date recording.

  • Straightness & runout inspection
  • Concentricity checking
  • Automated measurement systems
VMC Fixtures

High-precision VMC fixtures with pneumatic and toggle clamping systems for efficient machining operations on vertical machining centers.

  • Pneumatic clamping fixtures
  • Toggle clamp systems
  • Multi-component setups
Assembly Fixtures

Specialized assembly fixtures for metro engine parts and complex sub-assemblies ensuring accurate positioning and efficient production.

  • Metro engine parts assembly
  • Sub-assembly fixtures
  • Precision alignment tools
Precision Machining

High-precision machined components using advanced VMC machines with strict quality control and calibrated measuring instruments.

  • VMC/HMC/Jig boring operations
  • Surface grinding & drilling
  • Keyence precision measurement
